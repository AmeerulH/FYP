MUZICODES

Welcome to Muzicodes! Muzicodes is an interactive music based web application where you can plug in your own midi keyboard and play/create your own songs that are in the form of "Muzicodes". Playing these "Muzicodes" can trigger certain actions when you play your song.

Firstly, go to the photos page to upload any photos that you want to display when you are playing!

Secondly, go to the create page to create your "Muzicode" by putting the chords you want to use and select which image you want to display when pressing those chords.

Finally, go to the play page and select a song you want to play! Make sure you plug in your midi keyboard to your computer and enjoy!

Brief Information About Project

• Determine how the performing arts nurture skills such as thinking/cognitive, social, mathematical, reading and language skills at an early age?
